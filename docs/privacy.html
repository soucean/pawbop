Privacy Policy — PAW BOP

Effective date: July 14, 2026

We don't collect your data

PAW BOP has no accounts, no sign-in, and no forms. The app itself does not collect, store, or share any personal information. Game settings are saved only on your device.

The app shows ads via Google AdMob and uses Google Firebase (Analytics & Crashlytics) for anonymous usage statistics and crash reports. These services may automatically receive device identifiers (such as the advertising identifier) and usage data, which may be used to show ads. See Google's Privacy Policy and how Google uses data in AdMob.
