PAW BOP

A game made for cats to play

About

Fish, mice and cockroaches dart across the screen — your cat taps them with a paw to catch them. Pick the prey, adjust size and speed, put the device down and let your cat play. Best on a tablet.

Support

Questions, feedback or issues? Email us at devhaiho@gmail.com — we usually reply within a few days.

Tip for cat owners

Enable Guided Access (Settings → Accessibility) while your cat plays, so stray paws can't leave the app.
